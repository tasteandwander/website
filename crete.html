Appetizers

Dakos

Crisp barley rusk softened by juicy tomatoes, and topped with creamy mizithra. Simple, sun-soaked flavors.

Ingredients:

Burley rusks, tomatoes, extra virgin olive oil, myzithra cheese, Greek oregano, red onion, capers, salt and pepper.

Where to taste:

Chochlioi Boubouristi

Snails pan-fried until crackling and golden, kissed with vinegar and perfumed with fresh rosemary. Earthy, aromatic.

Ingredients:

Snails, flour, vinegar, olive oil, garlic, rosemary, salt, pepper.

Where to taste:

Apaki

Tender pork kissed with the aroma of rosemary. Each bite is smoky, and succulent—like a warm whisper from the mountains.

Ingredients:

pork (tenderloin or leg), coarse salt, pepper, cumin, oregano, thyme, laurel leaves, vinegar.

Where to taste:

Traditional Roman trattorias, especially in Trastevere

Kalitsounia

Little pastries embracing silky Cretan cheese and herbs, gently melting on the tongue.

Ingredients:

Pasta fillo, cheese, egg, butter, honey.

Where to taste:

Staka

Velvety, creamy sheep’s milk butter, gently cooked until luxuriously thick—warm, and indulgently savory.

Ingredients:

Creta sheep cream cheese.

Where to taste:

Tiropitakia

Golden, flaky pastries bursting with creamy cheese— a little pocket of savory pleasure.

Ingredients:

Fillo pastry, egg, butter, feta.

Where to taste:

Main Course

Kleftiko

Slow-cooked lamb wrapped in parchment or foil, infused with garlic, herbs, and olive oil—each bite meltingly soft, rich.

Ingredients:

Lamb, oregano, thyme, parsley, cloves, lemon, potatoes, vinegar, onion, feta.

Where to taste:

Xani

Tsigariasto Lamb

Slow-cooked lamb leg, infused with herbs and spices, each bite juicy and deeply savory—flavors that unfold luxuriously.

Ingredients:

Lamb, white wine, bay leaves, onion, garlic, salt.

Where to taste:

Boureki

Layers of zucchini, potato, and fragrant herbs, baked with molten cheese until golden and irresistible.

Ingredients:

Potatoes, courgette, feta, graviera, fillo sheets, mint, onion, oregano, salt, pepper.

Where to taste:

Desserts

Sfakiani pita

Thin pie filled with cheese, griddled until warm and drizzled with honey- each bite a perfect balance of sweet and creamy, soft and seductive.

Ingredients:

Flour, myzithra, olive oil, water, salt, raki, honey.

Where to taste:

Xani

Patouda

Fragrant pastries filled with crushed nuts, cinnamon, and honey —each bite a warm, aromatic burst of crunch and sweetness.

Ingredients:

Flour, egg, sugar, milk, raki, olive oil, honey, walnut, almond, sesame, nutmeg, dates.

Where to taste:

Sarikopitakia

Spirals flaky, warm, and irresistible, each bite a playful swirl of texture and flavor that seduces the senses.

Ingredients:

Flour, water, olive oil, vinegar, salt, mizithra, honey.
