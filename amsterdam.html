Snacks

Friets met mayo

Crisp fries, perfectly salted, each bite warm and satisfying—simple, indulgent comfort that hits just the right note every time.

Ingredients:

Potatoes and mayonnaise.

Bitterballen

Each bite teases the senses—crispy, golden outside, yielding to a warm, velvety, savory center that melts luxuriously on the tongue, leaving a lingering, indulgent pleasure.

Ingredients:

Stewing beef, water, onion, black peppercorns, bay leaf, cloves, fresh thyme, unsalted butter, shallots, all-purpose flour, milk, beef stock, unsweetened gelatin powder, water, salt, freshly ground black pepper, nutmeg, flat-leaf parsley, Dijon mustard, all-purpose flour, eggs, breadcrumbs, vegetable oil, Dijon mustard.

Where to taste:

Bars, Albert Cuypmarkt

Croquettes

Golden, crispy exterior, hiding a rich, savory, creamy filling that melts on the tongue—warm, indulgent, and utterly irresistible.

Ingredients:

Brisket, water, parsley, bay leaf, onion, beef bouillon cubes, unsalted butter, all-purpose flour, distilled white vinegar, yellow mustard, parsley, freshly ground nutmeg, powdered gelatin, room temperature water, salt and pepper, eggs, bread crumbs, milk.

Where to taste:

Albert Cuypmarkt
