Appetizers

Mondeghili

Golden-fried meatballs made from leftover boiled meat, breadcrumbs, and Parmesan, crispy on the outside and tender within.

Ingredients:

Boiled meat (beef or mixed), breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, eggs, mortadella, nutmeg, lemon zest, salt, pepper

Where to taste:

Traditional Milanese trattorias and osterie throughout Milan

Busecca

Velvety ribbons of tripe surrendering into a rich, soul-warming broth with tender beans—a dish that wraps around you like a blanket on a foggy evening.

Ingredients:

Pork belly, tomato sauce, white beans, Parmesan cheese, onion, celery, sage, butter, black pepper, carrot.

Where to taste:

Traditional Milanese trattorias and osterie throughout Milan

Insalata di nervetti

Velvety ribbons of tripe surrendering into a rich, soul-warming broth with tender beans—a dish that wraps around you like a blanket on a foggy evening.

Ingredients:

Veal trotters, carrots, celery, bay leaves, black peppercorns, salt, white wine vinegar, red onions, pickled peppers, parsley, extra virgin olive oil, black pepper.

Where to taste:

Traditional Milanese trattorias and osterie throughout Milan

First Course

Risotto alla Milanese

Creamy, golden risotto infused with saffron, butter, and Parmesan—the quintessential taste of Milan.

Ingredients:

Carnaroli rice, beef marrow, saffron, butter, Parmesan cheese, white wine, beef broth, onion

Where to taste:

Trattoria Milanese, Antica Trattoria della Pesa, traditional Milanese restaurants

Second Course

Cassoeula

A hearty winter stew of pork ribs, sausages, and savoy cabbage, slowly simmered until rich and comforting.

Ingredients:

Pork ribs, pork sausages, pork rind, savoy cabbage, carrots, celery, onion, tomato sauce, white wine

Where to taste:

Traditional Milanese trattorias, especially during winter months

Ossobuco alla Milanese

Tender veal shanks braised until melt-in-your-mouth soft, traditionally served with gremolata and saffron risotto.

Ingredients:

Veal shanks, flour, butter, white wine, beef broth, onion, carrot, celery, gremolata (lemon zest, garlic, parsley)

Where to taste:

Trattoria Milanese, Ratanà, classic Milanese restaurants

Costoletta alla Milanese

A breaded veal cutlet on the bone, fried in clarified butter until golden and crispy—Milan's pride.

Ingredients:

Veal cutlet with bone, eggs, breadcrumbs, clarified butter, salt

Where to taste:

Antica Trattoria della Pesa, Trattoria Milanese, traditional Milanese restaurants

Desserts

Panettone

A tall, dome-shaped sweet bread studded with candied fruits and raisins—Milan's most famous holiday gift to the world.

Ingredients:

Flour, eggs, butter, sugar, candied orange and citron, raisins, natural yeast (lievito madre), vanilla, honey

Where to taste:

Pasticceria Marchesi, Cova, Pavé, artisan bakeries throughout Milan
